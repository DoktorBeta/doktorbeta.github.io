Grammar

word order - subject, verb, object (SVO) - kanlomu eat bread
modifiers/adjectives AFTER object
for plurals, apply plural modifier BEFORE object
genders - animate, inanimate
funky pronoun copula. - "the ball is red" = "the ball he red" , "he is red" = "he red"
comparative [ big > bigger ] = big > more big, superlative [ big > biggest ] = big > best big
negation - "not _"
pronouns - i, he, it, you, i plural (we/us), he plural, it plural, you plural
articles - definite, indefinite. the, a/an
tense - present, progressive (-ing), past, future, conditional, perfect
aspect - simple, continuous (is happening), perfect (has happened and is not happening currently)
mood - subjunctive (wish/desire), imperative (commanding), potential (may happen)
passive voice switches word order to OVS - bread is eaten by kanlomu
cases - genitive (a wheel of cheese), possessive (john's book)
reflexive - "i wash myself" "he hurts himself"
reciprocal - "1 and 2 cut eachothers hair"
causative - "he raises his hand" = "he makes his hand rise", 'make' being the causative definirinator
questions yes/no - "do you wish to continue, yes or no?" no 'do', yes/no is defined by particle at end of sentence. "you wish to continue, yesno?"
questions interrogative - "where is the body?" questioning defined by particle at end of sentence, "the body is [where]" "you have eaten [what]"
